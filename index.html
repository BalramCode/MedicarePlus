Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our healthcare services

What are your clinic hours?

+

We're open Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM. We're closed on Sundays and major holidays. For urgent care outside these hours, please call our 24/7 emergency line.

How do I schedule an appointment?

+

You can schedule an appointment by calling our office at (555) 123-4567, using our online patient portal, or by filling out the registration form on this page. We typically can accommodate appointments within 48 hours for routine visits.

What insurance plans do you accept?

+

We accept most major insurance plans including Blue Cross Blue Shield, Aetna, Cigna, UnitedHealthcare, and Medicare. Please contact our billing department to verify your specific plan coverage before your visit.

Do I need to bring anything to my first appointment?

+

Please bring a valid photo ID, your insurance card, a list of current medications, and any relevant medical records from previous healthcare providers. Arriving 15 minutes early helps ensure we have time to complete necessary paperwork.

What services do you provide?

+

We offer comprehensive primary care including annual physicals, chronic disease management, preventive care, vaccinations, minor procedures, lab services, and referrals to specialists when needed. We also provide telehealth consultations for appropriate conditions.

How can I access my medical records?

+

You can access your medical records through our secure online patient portal. After your first visit, we'll provide you with login credentials. You can also request paper copies by contacting our medical records department at least 48 hours in advance.
